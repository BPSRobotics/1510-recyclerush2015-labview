Controller 1 (Drive):
-Left Joystick - forward, backward, left strafe, right strafe
-Right Joystick - pivot left, pivot right
-Right Trigger - hold to increase movement speed
-Left Trigger - hold to decrease movement speed

Controller 2 (Manipulator):
-Left Joystick - move lift up one level, move lift down one level
-Right Joystick - move lift up manually, move lift down manually
-A - move clamp in one width
-B - move clamp out one width
-X - move clamp in manually (ignores middle width beam breaker)
-Y - move clamp out manually (ignores middle width beam breaker)
