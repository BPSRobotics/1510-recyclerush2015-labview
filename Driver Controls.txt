Controller 1 (Drive):
-Left Joystick - forward, backward, left strafe, right strafe
-Right Joystick - pivot left, pivot right
-Right Trigger - hold to increase movement speed
-Left Trigger - hold to decrease movement speed
-Start Button - reset distance travelled

Controller 2 (Manipulator):
-Left Joystick (Up/Down) - move lift up one level, move lift down one level
-Right Joystick (Lef/Right) - move clamp in manually, move clamp out manually
-Y - move lift up one position
-A - move lift down one position
-X - move clamp in one position
-B - move clamp out one position
-Left Trigger - stop auto lift
-Right Trigger - stop auto clamp
